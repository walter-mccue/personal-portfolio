About Me

I'm Walter McCue

I already said I have a passion for building things. If you paid attention to my picture in the header or the content in my bucket list, you'd probably already guess that I'm really into trucks. What most people don't realize, is how much time I put into working on trucks and cars. I actually run my own small business installing aftermarket lighting on all makes and models. My goal is to one day open my own retail shop and provide a service for programming different lighting modes. So I decided to learn web development so I can set up an online retail space and begin my journey learning how to create digital web-based applications.

Optical Power and Performance

The above pictures showcase some of my skills. Anyone can slap a lightbar on a truck and hook up positive and negative leads to a battery. Because of that, I expanded into lighting for all vehicles. The picture on the left shows the driver's side headlight of a 2015 Ford Mustang. The Daytime Running Lights and headlight projector were both modified to run RGBW LEDs. Basically, that means they can be set to be any color of the rainbow and there's even a few preset patterns. The picture on the right shows a collage of pictures from a 2019 Dodge Charger. In addition to the headlights, I also added backlighting behind the grill/hood, underglow, and footwell lighting. All the lights are RGBW LED's like the other picture but they also sync up with each other. The features in both sets of pictures are for show only and are not legal for road use. Building custom lighting for customers like these two pictures is no easy task, and it is a skillset that few people have. I'm self-taught and have been working on cars since 2004. I have my own website at www.OpticalPowerPerformance.com and customers can email me there, place orders, or fill out the contact form and request a quote. Not included in the pictures is sound system installations. If you visit the website you'll notice that I offer that service as well. It's not as exciting as custom lighting but it's still fun to do and I enjoy helping my customers.
